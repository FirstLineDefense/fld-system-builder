# EDR-029 Final Phase 1 Foundation Summary

## Status
Accepted

## Date
2026-05-08

## Context

Phase 1 of the FLD platform architecture initiative focused on stabilizing and formalizing the engineering, governance, deployment, runtime, documentation, and intelligence foundations of the platform.

The FLD platform evolved rapidly from:

- experimental engineering tooling
- monolithic Flask workflows
- prototype optimization logic
- temporary deployment systems

into a governed engineering intelligence platform architecture.

The purpose of Phase 1 was to establish stable long-term architectural foundations before large-scale expansion.

## Phase 1 Objectives

Phase 1 focused on:

- architectural stabilization
- governance establishment
- deterministic engineering authority
- deployment reliability
- documentation continuity
- runtime isolation
- reporting modularization
- AI governance
- observability planning
- resilience strategy
- future scalability preparation
