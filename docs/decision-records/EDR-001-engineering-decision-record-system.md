# FLD Engineering Decision Record System

## Purpose

This folder stores Engineering Decision Records for the FLD System Builder platform.

Engineering Decision Records document:
- what decision was made
- why the decision was made
- what alternatives were considered
- what tradeoffs were accepted
- what future engineers need to understand

The goal is to preserve the architectural memory of the FLD platform so future engineers can understand, maintain, extend, or rebuild the system.

## Standard EDR Format

Each EDR should include:

1. EDR Number
2. Title
3. Status
4. Date
5. Context
6. Decision
7. Reasoning
8. Consequences
9. Future Considerations
10. Related Systems
11. Related Files
